# CheckPrep Privacy Policy

**Last updated: March 2026**

## Information Collection

CheckPrep collects minimal data necessary to provide the study experience. Your study progress, session history, and preferences are stored on your device. We do not collect personal information unless you explicitly provide it through feedback.

## Data Usage

Your study data is used solely to provide personalized study recommendations, track your progress, and improve the learning experience. We do not sell, trade, or share your personal data with third parties.

## Data Storage

All study progress and preferences are stored on your device using secure on-device storage. If you are signed in to iCloud, your study sessions and preferences may sync across your devices using Apple's iCloud and CloudKit services. This data remains within your personal iCloud account and is not accessible to us.

## Network Activity

CheckPrep connects to the following U.S. government services to provide study content and training features:

- **Aviation Weather Center** (aviationweather.gov) — live METAR, TAF, and PIREP weather data for training exercises
- **Federal Aviation Administration** (faa.gov) — Aeronautical Information Manual (AIM) content for reference and study
- **Electronic Code of Federal Regulations** (ecfr.gov) — Federal Aviation Regulations (FAR) for reference and study

No personal information is transmitted in these requests. As with any network connection, your device's IP address is visible to the respective servers. All connections use HTTPS encryption.

## iCloud Sync

If enabled, CheckPrep uses Apple's iCloud services to sync your study progress, bookmarks, and preferences across your devices. This data is stored in your personal iCloud account and is governed by Apple's privacy policy. We do not have access to your iCloud data.

## Third-Party Services

CheckPrep uses Apple's standard platform services including the App Store, CloudKit, and local notifications. These services are governed by Apple's own privacy policies. We do not integrate any third-party analytics, tracking, or advertising services.

## Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at **support.checkprep@icloud.com**.
