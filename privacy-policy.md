# CheckPrep Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** February 2026

## Information Collection

CheckPrep collects minimal data necessary to provide the study experience. Your study progress, session history, and preferences are stored locally on your device. We do not collect personal information unless you explicitly provide it through feedback.

## Data Usage

Your study data is used solely to provide personalized study recommendations, track your progress, and improve the learning experience. We do not sell, trade, or share your personal data with third parties.

## Data Storage

All study progress and preferences are stored locally on your device using secure on-device storage.

## Network Activity

CheckPrep connects to the Aviation Weather Center (aviationweather.gov) to fetch live METAR and TAF weather data for training purposes. No personal information is transmitted in these requests. As with any network connection, your device's IP address is visible to the server.

## Third-Party Services

CheckPrep may use Apple's standard services including the App Store and push notifications. These services are governed by Apple's own privacy policies. We do not integrate any third-party analytics or advertising services.

## Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at support.checkprep@icloud.com.
